Turning on Shaders

MMAMinecraft December 21, 2025
  1. Press ESC, click Options, click Video Settings
  2. Click Oculus on the left
  3. Choose one of the pre-loaded shader packs and click Apply.
  4. The game will now reload, but with shaders.

Bliss works with distant horizons for a truly breathtaking game, but you need dedicated graphics, and good ones at that!
