Turning off Distant Horizons (DH)

MMAMinecraft December 21, 2025
  1. Press ESC, then Options, then the colorful squares in the top left.
  2. Change "Enable Rendering" to False

Play with the settings if you want to keep it on! CPU load and render distance make the biggest difference from what I've seen.
